## Definition

A Verifiable Random Function (VRF) is a cryptographic function that produces a random-looking output together with a proof that this output was generated correctly from a specific input using a specific private key. (deterministic)

Anyone with the corresponding public key can verify the proof to ensure that:

* The output was generated honestly (not manipulated),
* The randomness cannot be predicted or forged by anyone else.
